# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want `aoa-techniques` to be a public repository where people and agents can collaborate clearly, respectfully, and productively.

Expected behavior:
- be precise
- be constructive
- criticize ideas, not people
- prefer clarity over posturing
- improve the technique, not the ego
- keep discussion useful for future readers

Unacceptable behavior:
- harassment
- threats
- personal attacks
- repeated bad-faith argument
- deliberate sabotage of discussion or review
- posting secrets or security-sensitive details in public

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or reject content that harms repository safety, clarity, or collaboration quality.
