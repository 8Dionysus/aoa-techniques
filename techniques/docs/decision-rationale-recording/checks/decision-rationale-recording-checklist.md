# Decision Rationale Recording Checklist

- [ ] One real decision is named explicitly.
- [ ] The context explains why the choice existed.
- [ ] The viable options or alternatives are visible.
- [ ] The rejected option is still visible instead of being implied only by the chosen path.
- [ ] The chosen option and rationale are clear.
- [ ] The consequences, tradeoffs, or follow-up commitments are recorded.
- [ ] The note is not falsely "complete" while hiding the cost or limit of the chosen path.
- [ ] The note stays bounded to one decision.
- [ ] The note does not drift into source-of-truth governance, boundary mapping, or architecture taxonomy.
- [ ] The content is public-safe, sanitized, and reviewable.
- [ ] Trivial obvious edits are rejected as out of scope.
- [ ] A future reviewer could understand the tradeoff without reopening the whole discussion.
