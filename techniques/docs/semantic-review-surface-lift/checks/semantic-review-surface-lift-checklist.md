# Semantic Review Surface Lift Checklist

Use this checklist to confirm that the review surface still behaves like a derived markdown lookup aid rather than a new decision engine.

- The authored semantic-review doc is still the source of review meaning.
- The derived surface can be regenerated from authored markdown.
- The lifted output preserves the cluster map, seam review, findings, and next-step summary.
- Consumers can route from the derived surface back to the authored review doc.
- The lift stays cluster-bounded instead of becoming a catch-all review bucket.
- The technique does not require scoring, status automation, or graph semantics to stay useful.
- Semantic review remains distinct from shadow review and from bundle-local adverse-effects notes.
