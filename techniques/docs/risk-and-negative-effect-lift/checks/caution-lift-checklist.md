# Caution Lift Checklist

Use this checklist to confirm that a caution-oriented lift still behaves like bounded markdown-first lookup rather than a new policy or metadata layer.

- The source bundle keeps caution meaning inside authored `## Risks`.
- `Failure modes`, `Negative effects`, `Misuse patterns`, `Detection signals`, and `Mitigations` stay explicit and distinct.
- Any derived caution-oriented read surface points readers back to the source markdown.
- The workflow does not add shadow metadata, caution IDs, generated caution outputs, or a new `kag` domain.
- The lift remains about bounded review and reuse questions, not scoring or policy enforcement.
