# stress-receipt-reground-closeout checklist

Use this checklist to verify that a bounded stress event stayed honest from
receipt through reviewed closeout.

- one bounded stress family and owner surface are named explicitly
- one owner-local receipt exists before broader interpretation starts
- the degraded continuation or explicit hold stays weaker than the normal route
- blocked widening is visible, not implied
- the next hop points to the owning layer rather than inventing a new judge
- any KAG or playbook adjuncts remain additive, not sovereign
- reviewed closeout records what was done, what stayed deferred, and what stayed blocked
- any exported eval bridge candidate is still evidence-only
