# minimal progression-evidence-lift

```yaml
progression_delta:
  movement:
    boundary_integrity: advance
    review_sharpness: advance
    proof_discipline: hold
    deep_readiness: reanchor
  verdict: advance-with-caution
  unlock_hints:
    - "safe to widen one session-harvest seam next"
```

Why this example stays bounded:

- it stays multi-axis instead of collapsing into one score
- it allows mixed movement across different axes
- it keeps unlock hints small and descriptive
