# minimal diagnosis-from-reviewed-evidence

```yaml
diagnosis_packet:
  symptoms:
    - "technique and skill language repeatedly collapsed together"
    - "review claims were made without stable proof anchors"
  probable_causes:
    - "owner-layer boundaries are being treated as convenience rather than law"
    - "review evidence is being compressed too early during recap"
  likely_owner_hints:
    - aoa-skills
    - aoa-evals
  suggested_repair_shape: bounded repair packet for owner-law and proof-law separation
  unknowns:
    - "unclear whether the root cause is one missing template or broader review habit drift"
```

Why this example stays bounded:

- it keeps symptoms, causes, and unknowns separate
- it names owner hints without treating them as final verdicts
- it suggests a repair shape without performing the repair
