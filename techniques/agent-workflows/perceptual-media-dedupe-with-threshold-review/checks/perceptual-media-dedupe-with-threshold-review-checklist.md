# perceptual-media-dedupe-with-threshold-review checklist

- [ ] the workflow groups near-duplicates without owning deletion or archive policy
- [ ] high-confidence groups and uncertain groups are visibly separated
- [ ] thresholds remain explicit and tunable
- [ ] group membership stays explainable enough for review
- [ ] semantic labeling and quality ranking stay out of scope
- [ ] the contract still makes sense across screenshots, memes, or photo variants without one-threshold dogma
