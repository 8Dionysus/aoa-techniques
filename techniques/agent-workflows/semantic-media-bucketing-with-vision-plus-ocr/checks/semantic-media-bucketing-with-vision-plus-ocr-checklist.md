# semantic-media-bucketing-with-vision-plus-ocr checklist

- [ ] the taxonomy is explicit, bounded, and small enough to review
- [ ] visual semantics remain primary and OCR stays a side-channel
- [ ] low-confidence or conflicting items route into review
- [ ] duplicate grouping, OCR extraction, and downstream action policy stay out of scope
- [ ] moderation, identity inference, and destructive automation remain outside the contract
- [ ] the bundle still makes sense across screenshots, memes, receipts, and `other` without turning into a catch-all media ontology
