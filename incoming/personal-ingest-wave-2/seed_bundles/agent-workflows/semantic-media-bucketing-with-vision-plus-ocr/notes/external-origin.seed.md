# external-origin seed - semantic-media-bucketing-with-vision-plus-ocr

## Donor spine

- CLIP
- PaddleOCR

## Bounded pattern extracted

- Bucket media with image-text semantics plus OCR side text so screenshots, memes, receipts, and other media classes remain reviewable under confidence gates.

## What stays out

- open-ended multimodal assistant claims
- hidden moderation policy
- identity or face inference

## Why narrower than the donors

- this seed extracts one reusable operational form
- it leaves implementation, runtime packaging, and donor worldview behind
- it does not claim the donor repo should be imported wholesale
